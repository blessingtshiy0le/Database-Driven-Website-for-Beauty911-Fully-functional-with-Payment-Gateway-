Treatment Information

The IV drip treatment is a non-invasive, intravenous infusion of vitamins, minerals, and other nutrients designed to provide various health benefits. The treatment is administered by a trained professional and typically takes 30-60 minutes to complete.

Risks and Complications

While IV drip treatments are generally considered safe, there are some potential risks and complications to be aware of:

  • - Allergic reactions to the ingredients
  • - Infection from improper needle insertion
  • - Nausea, dizziness, or headaches due to the infusion
  • - Temporary bruising or swelling at the injection site
